How to Stay Safe Online

Always verify the URL in your browser's address bar before entering sensitive information.

Look for HTTPS and a padlock icon, but remember that even phishing sites can use SSL certificates.

Hover over links before clicking to see the actual destination URL.

Use PhishShield's URL Inspector whenever you're unsure about a link's safety.
